Key Findings

  1. Vanilla is the winner over all the other flavors across all regions, with the highest sales in the Southern region at 312,000 units.
  2. The Southern region shows the highest total ice cream consumption with 788,000 units sold, while the Northern region has the lowest at 590,000 units.
  3. Strawberry is the least popular flavor in all regions, with sales figures consistently lower than both vanilla and chocolate, averaging only about 60% of vanilla's sales volume.
